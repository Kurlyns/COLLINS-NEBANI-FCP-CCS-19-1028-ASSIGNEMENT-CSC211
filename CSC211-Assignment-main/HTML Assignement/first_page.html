For over 50 years Brymo Academy has been providing World Class education to students across the world. Brymo Academy is ranked amongst the top ten universities in the UK, according to the Guardian university league table, and internationally amongst the top universities in the world. Brymo Academy is the only fully fledged branch campus in West Africa and is set to grow into a major regional teaching and research university of international standing in the near future. Our branch campus in Ghana offers the exact same degree to the degrees offered at our Brymo Academy main campus in the UK. What this means is that you will be awarded the same qualifications - as if you were studying at the main campus. Brymo Academy offers programmes in Business Management, Marketing, Politics, Economics, Taxation, Computer Science, Accounting & Finance and Law.
