IDANRE HILL

The Idanre hill or Oke Idanre is one of the historical tourist sites in Nigeria, it is located in the ancient town of Idanre, Ondo State. Idanre hill is one of the most beautiful known natural landscapes in Nigeria, there are so many cultural sites found within it, it also has great spiritual recognition among the people, as there are so many shrines and other spiritual places of worship within its territory. Idanre Hills is supposedly located on a Precambrian igneous batholith that is about 500 million years old and is cut by several large fractures that form deep valleys within the rocks.
